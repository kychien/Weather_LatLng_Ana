Weather Latitude Analysis

As one would naturally assume, there does seem to be a relationship between latitude and temperature. An initial concern was that I would have to try to pull data at different times to get the temperature at the same time of day to be more accurate, but it seems that difference in temperature between night and day isn't large enough of a factor to hide the overall relationship.

Before plotting the regression line, there also seemed to be a skew towards the northern hemisphere being colder. However, that just seems to be due to the lack of cities in the southern hemisphere to balance out the picture. It is interesting to note that the greatest deviations in results are around -20 and 40.
